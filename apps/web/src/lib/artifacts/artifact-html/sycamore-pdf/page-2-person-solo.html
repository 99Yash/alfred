Background

Sri spent 20+ years building enterprise platforms. At Atlassian, he was the first CTO, leading the cloud transformation that grew revenue from $500M to $2.5B+. He served on the boards of Splunk and SendGrid.

He holds a BS from Bangalore University, an MS in CS from Clemson, and an MS in Management from Stanford. After a stint as investor at Coatue, he founded Sycamore because he saw enterprises adopting AI fast but lacking governance infrastructure.
