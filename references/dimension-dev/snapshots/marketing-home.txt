--- notes ---
- The 7-tab use-case showcase is the cleanest articulation of the product story they have anywhere on the site. Each tab pairs a one-line elevator pitch with a single illustrated mock — no marketing fluff, no testimonials, no logo wall.
- The mocks are real-looking UI surfaces (not abstract illustrations). E.g. Catch Up's mock is the same conversation-thread chrome you see in the actual `/chat/<threadId>` view; Meeting Prep's mock matches what would live in the right rail's day-overview widget. So the marketing site is also a faithful preview of the app — useful since some surfaces (Deep Work in particular) aren't easily reachable in our authenticated capture.
- Login is **SSO-only** (footer link), with the primary "Get Started" CTAs presumably routing through the SSO flow too. No email/password option exposed publicly.
- The "Login with SSO" link is tucked in the footer, not the top nav — the top right is reserved for "Get Started" (sign-up). This is a sales-first nav.
